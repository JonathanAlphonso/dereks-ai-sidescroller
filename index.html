Procedural Sidescroller Arena

Survive waves of enemies spawned from both sides of the arena. Chain melee attacks to keep enemies at bay, and use the ground pound shockwave to control space.

A/D move, W or Space jumps, S ground pounds, J jab, K uppercut, L spike, U diagonal launcher. After defeat, smash the glowing Retry button (or press Enter/Space/R) to dive straight back into the neon mayhem.
